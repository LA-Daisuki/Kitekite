About the Room お部屋について

A Cozy Retreat for Baseball Fans

Step into a clean, thoughtfully furnished room designed with the baseball fan in mind. From the Dodgers-themed bedding to the framed World Series memorabilia on the wall, every detail reflects the spirit of LA's most beloved team.

The space features a comfortable queen bed, a private loft sleeping area, flat-screen TV, dedicated desk, ceiling fan, and air conditioning — everything you need for a relaxing and exciting stay in Los Angeles.

Natural light pours through sheer curtains, keeping the atmosphere bright and welcoming. Whether you're here for the big game or exploring the city, this is your perfect home base.
